How to play & Rules

RIQ

Each round consists of 10 questions, and you have 30 seconds per question to get your answer correct.
If you need, want or dare you can choose to get a clue which will be presented as a picture.
The clue may help you or even make you more confused that is one of the exiting thing about RIQ

How to start a new quiz & play

1) To start a new quiz just choose a difficulty level above

2) Once you have selected a difficulty level a new quiz starts, and the first question is presented to you.
Type in your answer, and if it's correct next question will be presented.
Otherwise keep guessing, as you have unimited tries to answer correcly until the time runs out

3) When a round i finished your result will be presented, and you can choose to either save your score to the scoreboard or not

4) Lastley you can start a new quiz with the same difficulty level, or navigate back to the home page and start a new fun quiz

Rules

The rules of RIQ is quite simple - answer correctly as fast a possible and you will earn more points,
but be aware if you decide to get a clue you will get fewer points
